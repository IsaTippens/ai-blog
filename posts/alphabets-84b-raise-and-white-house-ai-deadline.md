## III. DECENTRALIZED SPACE FINTECH

### 5. Google Backs Space Fintech \"Nebex\" in $30M Seed Round
Establishing the groundwork for automated orbital commerce, Google has backed New York-based space fintech startup [Nebex](https://www.fintechfutures.com/venture-capital-funding/nebex-30m-seed-round) in a **$30 million seed round**.
